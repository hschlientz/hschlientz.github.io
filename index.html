Dog Safety on the Boat

The safety of the dog is paramount when boating. Before the dog is allowed to swim, make sure that the propeller is off and the engine area has stopped running.

Always monitor your dog's exposure to the sun and make sure they are always hydrated.

Landlubber Dogs

Some dogs are not meant to boat. Any dog that cannot handle the heat (e.g., bulldogs) can find boating uncomfortable. Dogs can get seasick, so if your dog is drooling or vomiting, maybe they are more comfortable as a landlubber!
